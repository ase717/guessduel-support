Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GuessDuel handles information within the mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

GuessDuel does not require account creation or login.

The application stores limited, non-personal data necessary for gameplay and subscription features:

  • Anonymous unique identifiers (UUID)
  • Game credits
  • Subscription status (weekly or monthly)
  • Subscription expiration date
  • Credit reset timestamps

This data is securely stored using Supabase and is not linked to personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.

2. How We Use Information

  • Manage in-app credits
  • Determine premium access
  • Restore purchases
  • Ensure proper subscription functionality
  • Maintain app performance

We do not use data for advertising or cross-app tracking.

3. In-App Purchases

Subscriptions and payments are processed through Apple App Store. We do not collect or store payment information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent user data. Data may be processed by service providers strictly for operational purposes (e.g., Supabase, Apple).

5. Children’s Privacy

GuessDuel is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be posted on this page.
